A laptop, sometimes called a notebook computer by manufacturers, is a battery- or AC-powered personal computer (PC) smaller than a briefcase. A laptop can be easily transported and used in temporary spaces such as on airplanes, in libraries, temporary offices and at meetings. The convenience of a laptop over a desktop computer is its main advantage. The laptop can be moved easily from one location to other thanks to its lightweight, small size, and built-in battery. Many models are versatile and can be used at a park and managed to carry in a car. .
